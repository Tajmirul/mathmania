Question 1

Thirty percent of the members of a swimming club have passed the lifesaving test. Among the members who have not passed the test, 12 have taken the preparatory course and 30 have not. How many members are there in the swimming club? [Jamuna Bank MTO/PO-2017]

Solution:
Given that,
number of boy and girls in the ratio of 3:2
Suppose number of boys is 300 then no of girls will be 200
Scholar ship holder boys = 300x (20/100) = 60 boys
Scholarship holder girls = 200x (25/100) = 50 girls
Hence total 60+50 = 110 students are scholarship holders.
No of student who are not scholar=500-110=390
% of student who are not scholar =(390x100)/500 = 78%
Alternative Solution:
Let, the number of boys and girls be 3x and 2x respectively
Total students = 3x + 2x = 5x
The number of boys who are scholarship holder =20% of 3x = 3x / 5
The number of girls who are scholarship holder =25% of 2x
= 2x/4 = x/2
The number of students who 'does not get scholarship = 5x- {( 3x/5) + (x/2 )} = 78x / 20
% of students who does not get scholarship
